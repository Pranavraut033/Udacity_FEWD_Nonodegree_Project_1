German Shepherd

German Shepherd is the second-most popular dog breed by registrations (129,186) worldwide.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Lifespan: 9 – 13 years
  • Origin: Germany
  • Height: 55–65 cm

The German Shepherd is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. The breed's officially recognized name is German Shepherd Dog in the English language. The breed is also known as the Alsatian in Britain and Ireland.
